About Us

Plexus CPR is committed to providing high-quality training in life-saving skills. Our certified instructors bring years of healthcare and emergency response experience to ensure every participant leaves confident and prepared to act in critical situations. We offer internationally recognized certification that is widely accepted by healthcare institutions and workplaces.

Plexus CPR Center (PCC) is licensed by the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties and accredited by the Saudi Heart Association, located at Emaar Square - Jeddah.
