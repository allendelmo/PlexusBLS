Core Services

Our courses follow Saudi Heart Association Standards and are conducted by certified instructors using updated equipment and simulation-based training.

Basic Life Support (BLS)

Basic Life Support (BLS) is a level of medical care used in emergencies to maintain breathing and circulation, typically involving CPR, use of an AED, and basic airway management until advanced help arrives.

Duration: 4 hours

First Aid

First aid is the immediate care given to a person who is injured or suddenly ill, aimed at preserving life, preventing the condition from worsening, and promoting recovery until professional medical help is available.

Duration: 4 hours

Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS)

Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS) is a set of clinical interventions for the urgent treatment of cardiac arrest, stroke, and other life-threatening cardiovascular emergencies, including advanced airway management, medication administration, and ECG interpretation, typically performed by trained healthcare professionals.

Duration: 2 days

Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS)

Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS) is a specialized training program that teaches healthcare providers how to respond to critically ill or injured infants and children using advanced assessment, life-saving interventions, and team-based resuscitation techniques.

Duration: 2 days

Neontal Resuscitation Program (NLS)

The Neonatal Resuscitation Program (NRP) is a training course that equips healthcare providers with the skills and knowledge to perform effective resuscitation on newborns immediately after birth, focusing on airway management, ventilation, and support of circulation.

Duration: 2 days
