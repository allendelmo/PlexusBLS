Mission and Vision

Mission

  • To deliver comprehensive life support training tailored to healthcare professionals, institutions, and the community.
  • To maintain high-quality facilities and provide opportunities for collaboration and partnerships with like-minded organizations.
  • To support healthcare delivery through the availability of medical clinic spaces and office rentals dedicated to health-related services.

Vision

To become a leading center for life-saving education and healthcare excellence, fostering a safer and healthier community through training, collaboration, and innovation.

Our Facilities

  • Centrally located building with easy access and visibility.
  • Modern Classrooms and training halls with advanced audio-visual systems. Also, with high-speed internet connections.
  • High-quality manikins, AEDs, and training materials that meet international standards.
  • Dedicated rental office suites and medical clinic units.
  • Spacious reception and waiting areas for comfort and convenience.

About Us

Plexus CPR is a premier provider of Life Support Training Programs designed to equip individuals, professionals, and organizations with the essential knowledge and skills to respond effectively during medical emergencies.
