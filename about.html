About us

About Plexus CPR

Plexus CPR is a premier provider of Life Support Training Programs designed to equip individuals, professionals, and organizations with the essential knowledge and skills to respond effectively during medical emergencies. Strategically located in a high-profile, accessible area, our company offers state-of-the-art facilities, top-grade equipment, and nationally aligned training materials to ensure excellent learning experiences. We are not just a training center—we are a health and wellness hub, combining professional education, healthcare spaces, and collaboration opportunities under one roof. We have the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties (SCFHS) license number 4/90105 and Commercial Registration Certificate number 7010996499 from Ministry of Commerce under Al-Salama Training Center Company.

Mission and Vision

Mission

  • To deliver comprehensive life support training tailored to healthcare professionals, institutions, and the community.
  • To maintain high-quality facilities and provide opportunities for collaboration and partnerships with like-minded organizations.
  • To support healthcare delivery through the availability of medical clinic spaces and office rentals dedicated to health-related services.

Vision

To become a leading center for life-saving education and healthcare excellence, fostering a safer and healthier community through training, collaboration, and innovation.
